About Us

Centralized Academic Archive for JSS Students

Our Mission

The **JSS Previous Year Question Paper Archive** was created to address a simple challenge: the fragmentation of past exam resources. Our mission is to provide students of JSS Arts, Commerce, and Science College (Ooty Road, Mysore) with a centralized, modern, and highly accessible database where they can find previous year question papers and quality subject notes to aid in exam preparation.

What We Offer

  • Dynamic Search & Filters: Quickly locate exam question papers and subject study guides by filtering for Course (B.Sc, M.Sc), Subject, Exam Year, and Semester.
  • Safe Uploads: Contributors can easily share PDF exam papers with the community. Every file is validated through security checks (magic numbers and UUID renaming) to keep the archive safe.
  • Quality Moderation: Administrators review each upload in a queue to ensure only high-quality, relevant study materials become public.

Get Involved

This archive thrives on peer-to-peer contributions. If you have exam papers from previous semesters or neat study notes, please upload them! Let's work together to help our college community achieve higher academic success.
